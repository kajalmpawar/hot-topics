GMO food

Humans have been modifying food and food ingredieents for thousands of years now. Methods like selective breeding and cross-breeding have been used to create desirable traits in plants and animals. GMO, Genetically Modified Organisms, is used to describe foods created through the process of genetic engineering. Genetic engineering involves: identifying the genetic information of an organism, retriving that information, inserting that information into the DNA of another organism and then growing that organisation. Scientists are constantly creating and developing new ways and methods to create new varieties of plants and animals using genome editing. There are several tools used for this process to make crops more nutritious, tolerant to climate change and resistant to pests. (FDA, 2020)
